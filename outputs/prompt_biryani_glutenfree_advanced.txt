Title:    Chicken Biryani
Cuisine:  South Asian
Servings: 6

Ingredients (12):
- 1 kg bone-in chicken thighs
- 500 g basmati rice
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 200 g whole-milk yogurt
- 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp saffron soaked in 50 ml warm milk
- 1 bunch fresh coriander and mint
- 4 tbsp ghee
- 1 tsp wheat flour (for sealing the lid)
- Salt to taste

Original steps:
1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
2. Fry the onions in ghee until deep golden, then reserve half for layering.
3. Parboil the basmati rice in heavily salted water with whole spices until 70% cooked, then drain.
4. Layer rice over the chicken in a heavy pot, top with fried onions, saffron milk, and chopped herbs.
5. Seal the lid with a wheat-flour dough rope and cook on dum (low heat) for 25 minutes.
6. Rest for 10 minutes before opening and gently fluffing the rice.
